Our Story

Established in 2019 by a team of industry experts with extensive experience in designing and developing web, mobile, and desktop applications, Lexogrine has maintained a steadfast focus on providing expertise in cutting-edge scalable technologies. Our technology choices stem from our deep commercial experience. We have dedicated ourselves to upholding quality, transparency, and open-mindedness to ensure the creation of an environment where individuals thrive and clients achieve success.

Having established JavaScript and TypeScript as our foundational technology stack, we made this choice to ensure a clean codebase and easy knowledge sharing among the development team. Consequently, we centered our efforts on React, React Native, Node.js, and Electron. Since then, we have further broadened our expertise to ensure the delivery of optimal solutions to our clients based on their individual needs.

Thanks to our experience and commitment in just 3 years we managed to deliver more than 50 digital products and built our own successful software solution - Lexogrine HUD Manager - which in less than a year became an esports industry standard.
